Why "native" matters

Other path add-ons invent their own tokens. Open the file on a render farm or a teammate's machine without the add-on, and the path breaks.

Zap Output is different — Blender's own tokens ({scene_name}, {camera_name}, ...) do the work, and dates/versions are stamped as real text (260713, v001).

Delete the add-on and every path still renders exactly the same. Farm-safe. Team-safe.
